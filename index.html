Could we live without the Internet?

The Internet has become an essential and unavoidable part of our life. Nowadays, almost nothing gets done without it. However, could our blind dependence in it be a threat to ourselves? Are we investing too much in this new medium that we are risking to lose too much if we ever were to live without it? Could this huge infrastracture we call the Internet be something momentary in human history? Is it possible that somehow it may not be around in the near future? Most people go about their daily lives as if Internet has always been here and always will be. However, its success and mere presence are not proof of its permanence. It would be unwise to think it will always be around. Actually, we have no guarantee that it will. It is evident that it is so reliable but yet at the same time it is so vulnerable.
